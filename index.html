Follow these simple steps to use RedZHub scripts
1 Find the script you want to use
2 Click the "Copy To Clipboard" button
3 Open your Roblox executor (Synapse X, KRNL, etc.)
4 Paste the copied script into your executor
5 Click "Execute" in your executor
6 Enjoy the script features in your game!
Important Notes:
  • Make sure your executor is up to date
  • Some scripts may require specific executors
  • If a script doesn't work, try rejoining the game
  • Scripts are regularly updated to match game updates
About RedZHub Scripts

RedZHub Scripts is a collection of high-quality scripts for popular Roblox games. Our scripts are designed to enhance your gameplay experience with useful features and automation.

This extension provides easy access to our most popular scripts, allowing you to quickly copy and use them in your favorite games.

Our scripts include:

  • Blox Fruits scripts with auto farming and more
  • Blade Ball scripts with auto parry and other features
  • Pet Simulator scripts for auto farming and hatching
  • Meme Sea scripts with various game enhancements
  • And many more!

All scripts are regularly updated to ensure compatibility with the latest game versions.
